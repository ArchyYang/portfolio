MAZE WORLD

Train the agent getting maximum rewards in a grid world by leveraging knowledge Reinforcement learning. The domain consists of a 10x10 grid of cells. The agent being controlled is represented as a red square. The goal is a yellow oval and the agent can receive a reward of 1 for reaching it and ending the current episode. Blue squares are pits which yield a penalty of -10 and end the episode. Black squares are walls which cannot be passed through. If the agent tries to walk into a wall they will remain in their current position and receive a penalty of -3.

Algorithms used: TD-Lambda, Expected-SARSA, Double Q-learning.
